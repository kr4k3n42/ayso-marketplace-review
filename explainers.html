How the economics work

AYSO National negotiates the sponsor budget and retains a management share for program administration, sponsor management, reporting, and fulfillment support.

Sponsor budget100%
National management share20%
Placement budget80%

The Region-facing marketplace shows approved offer values—not confidential sponsor negotiations. Estimated Region value is the selected per-uniform price multiplied by uniform count, capped by available placement budget.
